About JMT Music

A focused catalog and creative production practice built for artists, filmmakers, and media teams.

JMT Music is the production label and music library of Jonathan Tripp, creating original beats, instrumental music, and sync-ready compositions.

The catalog spans modern hip hop, lo-fi, cinematic textures, and contemporary production for artists, filmmakers, and media creators.

Each release is developed as part of a cohesive body of work: distinctive enough to stand alone, structured to support a voice, and clear enough to serve a larger story.
