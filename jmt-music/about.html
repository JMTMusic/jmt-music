About JMT Music

Independent production with a clear point of view, made for artists and visual storytellers.

JMT Music is the production label and music library of Jonathan Tripp, creating original beats, instrumental music, and sync-ready compositions.

The catalog spans modern hip hop, lo-fi, cinematic textures, and contemporary production for artists, filmmakers, and media creators.

Every track begins with feel: tactile drums, considered space, memorable texture, and an emotional center that can live on its own or support a larger story.
